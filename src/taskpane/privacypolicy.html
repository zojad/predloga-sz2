Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-05-27

This Privacy Policy explains how the Preposition Checker Word add-in handles and protects any information you provide when you use the add-in.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, user content, or document data. All processing happens locally in your Word application.

2. Use of Information

Since no data is collected or transmitted, we do not use your information in any way.

3. Data Security

Because the add-in does not send or store data externally, there are no additional security measures beyond those in your version of Microsoft Word and your local device security.

4. Third-Party Services

This add-in does not call any external web services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

5. Children’s Privacy

This add-in is intended for users of all ages, including children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date and notify you via the Support page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at predlogasz@gmail.com.
