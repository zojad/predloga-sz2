Getting Started

  1. Install from the Office Store
    • Word on the web: Click Add-ins, search for Pregledovalnik predlogov, then click Add.
    • Word desktop: Go to the Home tab and click Add-ins → Get Add-ins, search for Pregledovalnik predlogov, then click Add.
  2. Use the add-in
    • In the task pane, click Check Prepositions to highlight any incorrect s, z, h or k prepositions in the body, headers, and footers.
    • Click Accept All to apply corrections or Reject All to clear the highlights.
